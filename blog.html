Today we will show you how to make a tasty ramen dish for one, while working under a budget of $5.00.

Ingredients

  • Ramen: $0.25 for 1 package
  • Eggs: $2.00 for 12
  • Smoked Sausage: $1.55 for 7 oz
  • Green Onions: $0.50 for 5.5 fl oz
    • Bay Leaves: $0.69 for 0.2 oz

Directions

First, prepare the ingredients. Chop green onions and slice the sausage. Next, make sure to boil the ramen in a medium size pot with 2 cups of water with one or two bay leaves. Add broth and stir. Add the smoked sausage and green onions to boiling ramen and stir again. After five minutes, turn off burner and pour ramen into a large dinner bowl. Let simmer. In a separate pot, boil an egg until it is semi hardened. Once finished boiling, peel and let cool. After the egg has cooled slice in half and add to bowl of ramen. Then, enjoy!
