About

Secret Family Recipes Cookbook has been in business for 50 years, helping cooks, chefs, bakers, and more, access their private recipes. Early on we recognized the power of the web and its capability for storage and retrieval. Initially Secret Family Recipes Cookbook was a personal database for Momma May. We then realized that this could be a great tool for others and opened the website to the public. We pride ourselves on being easy to use, safe and secure. Whether you are in the industry or an at home chef, we are here for you.
